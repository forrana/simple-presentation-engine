Why it's so importent?

If we don't have present perfect we will suffer in the pain to explain current moments of our life, e.g.

He studied all night (before exams) vs He has studied up to now (And he is exosted)

He spill his coffee vs He has spilled his coffee (at that moment)

He didn't see the ocean ( becouse he moved to middle of continent) vs (at that moment)

So, as we can see, it can be usefull at some situations

And we can't just skip it.

We even have to use it from time to time, without any options.
